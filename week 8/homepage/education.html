My university

Aligarh Muslim University (AMU), established in 1920, is a premier central university located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh. It was originally founded as the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College in 1875 by Sir Syed Ahmad Khan to promote modern education among Indian Muslims. AMU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including engineering, medicine, law, arts, and sciences. The campus is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant student life, and historic architecture. It attracts students from all over India and abroad, fostering a diverse academic environment. AMU plays a significant role in the educational and socio-cultural development of the country.
